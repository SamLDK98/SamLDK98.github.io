The hypotenuse is always the radius — it never changes length. The legs trade off as the point turns. Every right-triangle shape appears once between 0° and 90°, and the area is largest at 45°.
Switch on the trace and sweep counterclockwise from (1, 0): every 22.5° a faint copy is left behind, until sixteen triangles ring the circle and you can see the shape morph all the way round.
